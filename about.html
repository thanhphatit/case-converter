About All Caps to Lowercase

Our Story

All Caps to Lowercase was founded in 2023 with the goal of providing a fast, easy, and accurate way to convert text case online. We are a team of developers and designers dedicated to creating useful and user-friendly tools.

Over the past year, we have helped thousands of users convert text case for their websites, documents, and social media posts. Our commitment to simplicity and efficiency has been the driving force behind our success.

Our Team

Our team consists of two developers and one designer. We come from diverse backgrounds in web development, software engineering, and graphic design.

Together, we work collaboratively to create and maintain our text case conversion tool. We are constantly learning and improving our skills to provide the best possible experience for our users.

Our Approach

At All Caps to Lowercase, we believe in simplicity and efficiency. We strive to provide a clean, intuitive interface that allows users to quickly and easily convert text case without any fuss.
